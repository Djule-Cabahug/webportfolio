E-Commerce

E-commerce is the process of purchasing and selling goods and services via the internet. It covers various online commercial activities and facilitates the electronic exchange of products and services without time or location constraints. E-commerce transactions can be B2B, B2C, C2C, or C2B. The expansion of the internet and mobile technology has greatly boosted e-commerce, enabling seamless online interactions and transactions between businesses and consumers.

Online Booking System

An online booking system is a digital tool that lets customers book and pay for services online. It helps businesses handle reservations, appointments, and schedules through electronic means. Customers can check availability, book services, and get instant confirmations. Widely used in sectors like travel, hospitality, events, and healthcare, these systems enhance efficiency, cut down manual tasks, and offer a smooth booking experience. They often feature automated reminders and built-in payment options.
